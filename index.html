Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres

The protrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neocalssical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimesions: 100 x 70cm
Home: Musée du Louvre, Paris
